Blog

Everyone says it, but time really is fleeting. When I was in kindergarten, five minute time outs seemed unbearable, but now, weeks fly by with the blink of an eye. To truly appreciate life and savor the moment, I hope to detail the various intricacies in of my through this blog. I hope that through reflection, I will learn to live in the present and realize my mistakes sooner rather than later. Ultimately, this blog represents some of my greatest values: Writing, Reflection, and Empathy

Sure, things are far easier said than done and I already have plenty of committments on my plate, but I hope to post every other week. Some may find my writing too personal or irrelavent, but at least 10 years from now, I can look back and relive.
